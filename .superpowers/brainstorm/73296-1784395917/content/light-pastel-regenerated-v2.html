Keep surfaces light. Panels, cards, inputs, toolbar buttons, and menus are white or very pale theme tints.
No yellow fallback. Runtime palette excludes yellow and avoids warm unrelated accents.
Theme-relative contrast. Text is a darker version of the chosen pastel; active states are saturated but still soft.
Same layout. Full-width toolbar, side panels, resize handles, drawer tabs, and canvas placement stay the same.
